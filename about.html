Company Story

Bubbles Cleaning Company was founded in 2005 with a single mission in mind: to provide exceptional cleaning services to residential and commercial clients. The company's story began when its visionary founder, Sarah Johnson, recognized the need for reliable and eco-friendly cleaning solutions in the market. Driven by her passion for cleanliness and sustainability, Sarah assembled a team of dedicated professionals who shared her vision. Together, they worked tirelessly to establish Bubbles Cleaning Company as a leading provider of environmentally conscious cleaning services. Over the years, Bubbles Cleaning Company has grown exponentially, expanding its client base and range of services. Today, the company proudly serves thousands of satisfied customers, offering a comprehensive suite of cleaning solutions tailored to meet each client's unique needs. With a commitment to quality, innovation, and environmental stewardship, Bubbles Cleaning Company continues to set the standard in the cleaning industry.

Eco-Friendly Approach

At Bubbles Cleaning Company, we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact while delivering exceptional cleaning results. We take great pride in our eco-friendly approach, utilizing sustainable products and employing environmentally responsible practices. Our dedicated team undergoes continuous training to stay informed about the latest advancements in green cleaning techniques. We strive to reduce waste, conserve water and energy, and promote the use of biodegradable and non-toxic cleaning agents. By incorporating eco-friendly practices into our operations, we aim to create a healthier living and working environment for our clients and contribute to a sustainable future. Some of our eco-friendly practices include:

  • Using certified green cleaning products that are free from harmful chemicals and toxins.
  • Implementing microfiber cleaning technology to minimize the use of disposable cleaning materials.
  • Optimizing our cleaning processes to reduce waterconsumption and waste generation.
  • Partnering with local recycling facilities to ensure proper disposal of waste materials.
  • Promoting energy-efficient practices, such as using eco-friendly equipment and appliances.
  • Encouraging our clients to adopt sustainable practices and offering tips on maintaining a clean and eco-friendly space.

By choosing Bubbles Cleaning Company, you can trust that your cleaning needs will be met with a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.
